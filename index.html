Redefining the Hacker Stereotype

TechTogether works to elevate the access, exposure, and inclusion of gender-marginalized students in the interdisciplinary fields of technology. We aim to inspire our community and provide them with the confidence to pursue their curiosities.

TechTogether was originally founded in 2017 as SheHacks by a group of 13 students across Boston. What was once a one-time event has transformed into a nonprofit after the founders realized the impact gender-focused events had on gender-marginalized communities. Now, TechTogether has extended from Boston to other major cities nationwide, including Seattle!

Become an Organizer for TechTogether Seattle

Oversee and implement the creation of all TechTogether websites and web applications. This includes creating and building a full-stack event page and live site, and being the point of contact for anything web-based through the organizing process!

Assist the director in event planning and oversee day-to-day operations. You’ll be in charge of tasks the logistics that help will make TechTogether Seattle possible. You will be helping with managing the financial budget, recruiting enough volunteers & mentors for the event, and working with sponsors to confirm workshops.

Build and maintain relationships with the community to secure funding, speakers, workshop leaders, etc. This includes reaching out to sponsors and communicating with community members to make sure we have the support we need to put together an awesome experience for our hackers!

Oversee recruitment, organization, and implementation for the event. You will help organize the event with a focus on making TechTogether inclusive, fun, and an overall great experience for every hacker who participates. This includes finalizing activities for the hackers, securing volunteers, mentors, and judges, and selecting challenges and prizes.

Lead and implement all marketing and communication activities on behalf of TechTogether to ensure brand consistency. This includes creating engaging written content on social media platforms, e-newsletters, and web pages. You will also help with creating the theme for the event, relaying information about the event to the community, and increasing awareness and enthusiasm for our hackathon!

Create and maintain a strong social media presence through eye-catching illustrations and graphics. This includes creating social media graphics such as Instagram posts, Facebook banners, and YouTube thumbnails. You will also assist in the production of print marketing such as flyers, postcards, stickers, etc. You'll collaborate with the marketing team to develop, grow, and expand our marketing channels to a wider audience.
